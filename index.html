About

Hello! I am currently a fourth-year Undergraduate student in the Computer Science at Yonsei University.

My research focuses on developing Generative models for Audios including Music and Singing Voice that ensure Naturalness and harmony for real-world applications.

  • How to apply Generative models that show excellent performance in CV and NLP to get high-quality generated audio?
  • How to design any-to-any VC models to enhance Intelligibility, speaker-similarity and Naturalness?

In particular, below are some keywords of my recent interests:

  • Singing Voice Conversion
  • Audio Synthesis
  • Speech Recognition
  • Text-guided Image Generation

I am actively looking for a graduate position for Fall 2024!
